Changing Width, Height, and Position of Square Corresponding to The position of a persons hands and nose.

NOTE - Your left and right hands must be visable aswell as your nose.
To change the size of the Square Move your hands farther appart to make it bigger, and closer together to make it smaller.
If you want to change the position of the square just move nose and the square will follow.
